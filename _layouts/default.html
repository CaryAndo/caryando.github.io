About Me

Hi! My name is Cary Anderson and I’m an aspiring programmer (code ninja) in Southern California. I have experience with Android programming, desktop application programming with QT and wxWidgets. I have worked with sockets in Python and Ruby, (though I am no longer fluent in the latter) and I have a very good understanding of how the Internet protocol suite works. Lately I have been getting more and more into Android game programming, so many posts are likely to concern that. I am a firm believer that FOSS is the best thing to ever happen to technology and that it should always be used over proprietary software. This is my blog where I like to put blog-like things, and express half-baked ideas in a “bloggy” sort of way. I hope that the things I put here can be helpful, interesting, or entertaining… Thanks for the visit.
